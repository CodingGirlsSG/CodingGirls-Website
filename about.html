We believe that computer programming and other technical skills are a tool of empowerment,

CodingGirls aims to close the gender gap in technology, we aim to inspire girls to be confident, passionate and successful in technology by showing real life models, offering them learning resource and networking opportunities



Why we do what we do?

Computing is the future, we believe that programming and other technical skills are a tool of empowerment. However, in US, only 18% of computer science majors are women. When it comes to work, the number is worse.
